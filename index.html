Committee Information · Spring 2015
Spark SC Committees are your opportunity to get involved with the planning and execution of some of the largest events and initiatives on campus. We're looking for talented student leaders to join our team and help us do great things.
Open Committees

TechLA Internships

Help connect students to internships in Los Angeles. Build the TechLA brand, familiarize yourself with LA companies and recruiters, and promote Los Angeles as an entrepreneurial hub.

TechLA Startup Career Fair

Our flagship event in the spring semester, the TechLA Startup Career Fair, is bringing out top LA startups to connect with students in mid-February. Work on outreach and logistics to help strengthen USC’s connection to the startups in Los Angeles.

LA Tech Tours

This is your opportunity to get connected to the growing companies in Los Angeles –– reach out to founders, build relationships with them, and set up “field trips” all throughout the LA startup community.

Hack Nights

Our weekly Hack Nights need a new approach –– the new model for bringing together USC’s hackers and makers, and create something they can look forward to each week.

Arts, Grooves, and Foods

An event that seeks to facilitate innovative discussion and ignite the entrepreneurial aspirations of students from multiple disciplines. The event will promise thought provoking conversation, mouthwatering food, and soulful tunes. You will get to work on attracting the most interesting and creative entrepreneurs in the artistic, culinary, and musical fields.

Community Service Project

Design and manage a social entrepreneurship project that engages with the larger Los Angeles community in collaboration with the organization No Right Brain Left Behind.

Student Features

Identify and showcase incredible USC students doing fascinating work on Spark SC’s social media.

Spark SC Blog

Create content that engages with our community and highlights the awesome students, groups, and initiatives under the Spark SC umbrella. We are shifting away from newsletters and to blog posts to keep our content relevant and exciting.

Spark SC Marketing

Create and spread Spark SC’s vision. Design marketing campaigns and promotions so that the whole campus can see what we’re up to and get involved. See your work plastered on builtins and event pages.

Spark SC Media

Spark SC has events that bring hundreds of students together and push USC’s entrepreneurial culture; yet, we have very little photo/video content to show for it. Help us capture the excitement by being in the “first class” of our media team through photography and videography.
