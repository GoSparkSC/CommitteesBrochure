Committee Information · Spring 2016
Open Committees

TechLA Internships

Help connect students with paid internships in Los Angeles. Build the TechLA brand, familiarize yourself with LA companies and recruiters, and be the interface between USC and the LA startup community.

Startup Career Fair

Our flagship event in the spring semester, the TechLA Startup Career Fair, is bringing out top LA startups to connect with students in mid-February. Work on outreach and logistics to help strengthen USC’s connection to the startups in Los Angeles.

LA Tech Tours (Safari)

This is your opportunity to get connected to the growing tech scene in Los Angeles – reach out to founders, build relationships with companies, and set up "field trips" all throughout the LA startup community.

USC Hackers

Make an impact in the USC Computer Science community by representing USC Hackers, and throwing large-scale events that help lower the barriers to entry to hacking at USC.

Arts, Grooves, and Foods

Arts, Grooves, and Foods is a celebration of creative entrepreneurship in the artistic, musical, and culinary fields. Help us ignite the entrepreneurial aspirations of students from multiple disciplines, during a night of thought provoking conversation, soulful tunes, and mouthwatering food.

Design Workshops

There's not much happening on campus that addresses the design community - we're looking to change that. Spark SC Design Workshops will take you through many key elements of the design process, and explore different facets of design.

Community Service Project

Design and manage a social entrepreneurship project that engages with the larger Los Angeles community in collaboration with No Right Brain Left Behind.

Blog

Create content that engages with our community and highlights the awesome students, groups, and initiatives under the Spark SC umbrella. We are shifting away from newsletters and to blog posts to keep our content relevant and exciting.

Spark SC Dev Team

Create content that engages with our community and highlights the awesome students, groups, and initiatives under the Spark SC umbrella. We are shifting away from newsletters and to blog posts to keep our content relevant and exciting.

Spark SC Marketing

Create and spread Spark SC’s vision. Design marketing campaigns and promotions so that the whole campus can see what we’re up to and get involved. See your work plastered on builtins and event pages.

Spark SC Media

Spark SC hosts events that bring hundreds of students together and promote a culture of entrepreneurship at USC. Help us capture the excitement by being in the "first class" of our media team through photography and videography.

Spark SC Graphics

The Spark SC Graphics team handles all of our graphics for our events and initiatives. Help define Spark's outward appearance and further our mission by producing deliverables for Spark's various brands.

Podcast

The Spark SC podcast highlights outstanding faculty, alumni, and student entrepreneurs in entertaining 20 minute long podcast episodes. Help us plan, record, and produce great audio content that gets published to the iTunes podcast store.
